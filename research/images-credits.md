  (dark coleus) is a stand-in, not the real species.

  separate, confidently-labelled azalea image.
- **Espalier fruit tree** — nothing verifiable. `cover_issue_03` (potted lemon trees) and
  `plant_fig` / `plant_peach` carry the "fruit trees shrunk" idea instead.
- **A true compost heap / no-dig bed** — Unsplash's only "compost heap" results are all
  Unsplash+ (paid). We use hands-in-compost (`technique_compost_hands`) and
  `technique_raised_bed` instead.
- **A literal soil-test kit / probe** — not available. `technique_soil_lab` (lab researchers
  with a plant) and `technique_plant_microscopy` stand in for the "science" beat.
- **A styled potting-bench flat lay** — `technique_potting_bench` (boots, gloves, hand tools)
  is the closest verified option; it is not a bench.
